Jenny Kim is a multidisciplinary designer and curator based in New York.

She holds a BFA in Communications Design with a focus on Graphic Design from Pratt Institute.

Her practice is focused on 3D visuals, branding, identity systems, motion, typography, and web projects.

In her spare time, she likes to experiment with woodworking, textiles, and analog technology.

愛 draws inspiration from Facebook's research involving two chatbots, Bob and Alice. As their dialogue became unintelligible to human translation, this animation reimagines the phenomenon as two computers forming a romantic connection. The title, 愛 (ài), means love in Japanese while also reflecting the acronym for artificial intelligence.
